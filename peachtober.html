PEACHTOBER 2022

Hosted by: Sha'an d'Anthes


Created by Jake Parker in 2009, Inktober was created as a challenge to improve his inking skills and develop positive drawing habits. It has since grown into a worldwide endeavor with thousands of artists taking on the challenge every year. Every October, artists all over the world take on the Inktober drawing challenge by doing one ink drawing a day for the entire month. Sha'an d'Anthes (known as @furrylittlepeach online) is one of the many artists who created their own prompt list.

FROM THE CREATOR

"Peachtober is a time for play, a time to share your work with those following along and a time to find a bunch of new artists to appreciate and connect with. This is a creative prompt list with no limits on medium or style - over the last 5 years of hosting this challenge I've been thrilled to witness entries of all kinds including drawings, paintings, digital illustrations, textiles, 3D work, pottery, animation, make-up looks, photography, poetry, short stories and more! I'm so excited to see what we all have in store for eachother this year."
